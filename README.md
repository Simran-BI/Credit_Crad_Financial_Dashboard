# Credit_Crad_Financial_Dashboard
Poower BI Dashboard
Project objective: 
To develop a comprehensive credit card dashboard that provides real time insights into key performance metrics and trends, enabling stakeholders to monitor and analyze credit card operations effectively.

DAX used:
. SWITCH()
. DIVIDE()
. CALCULATE()
. SUM()


Insights: Overview YTD
. Overall Revenue is 55M.
. Total interest is 7.8M.
. Total Transaction Amount is 44.5M.
. Highest contributor of revenue are businessmen contributing 17M of the total revenue.
. Male are contributing more than females in revenue,i.e. male= 35M and female= 20M.
. Card categories are Silver, Blue, gold and Platinum which are contributing as follows 6M,46M,2M and 1M respectively out of the total revenue.
. Top 3 contributing states are TX, NY and CA.
. Most of the contributing are making use of swipe facility.
